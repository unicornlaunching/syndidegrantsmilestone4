Purpose

The purpose of Syndi The Community Co-Pilot is to:

  • Accelerate the go-to-market of key projects and founders in the Stacks ecosystem using AI to accelerate recruiting, onboarding, ideation, prototyping, and product development.
  • Level up the awareness and capabilities of ecosystem participants to use AI to accelerate their own growth.

E. Charisma.btc Security Concern Response

During the building process, Charisma.btc, a staking platform on stacks, experienced an exploit where hundreds of thousands of dollars were stolen in a matter of minutes. The Syndicate held a number of spaces during the exploit to help stacks ecosystem members manage their exposure and learn about the exploit itself.
